What is MORE-TO-WIN™?

MORE-TO-WIN™ enables Dealers to focus on the right audience with meaningful conversation using predictive data science. As a data marketing solution, MORE-TO-WIN™ identifies and targets active buyers in your local market who are looking to purchase a new vehicle within a short time period – using unique data touch points that separate you from your competitors.

Stop the guessing and marketing to the masses. MORE-TO-WIN™ enables dealers to market smarter and reduce unnecessary marketing waste. Deliver timely and relevant messages when they matter the most. Best of all, it’s easy and requires minimal time invested by your team.

How does it work?

As a MORE-TO-WIN™ customer you’re given exclusive territory rights as a radius around your dealership. The MORE-TO-WIN™ system monitors thousands of data sources – both online and offline behaviors, buying habits, financial and vehicle data to identify consumers around your dealership. It gives you the highest quality of leads.

Now, we put our target marketing skills to work – with both digital and direct mail campaigns. Our creative marketing team will design a campaign to designed to capture their attention and encourage dealership engagement. On a monthly basis, our Digital Marketing Specialists will meet with you to provide a ROI analysis and metric report.

Why we are different?

We don’t data mine from your DMS to find active buyers. We use predictive analytics to find active consumers within a custom personalized radius of your dealership utilizing data science and MORE-TO-WIN™ propriety algorithms. We identify and market to ready-to-buy individuals based on thousands of key data analytics combined with digital display, email and direct mail marketing. Our approach includes analyzing the propensity to buy based on data science related to consumer online and offline behavior, enabling us to rank, target and focus on the top 10% consumers.

MORE-TO-WIN™ targets 60-70% new leads and 30-40% returning customers that contribute to your retention initiatives.

With customers actively in the sales funnel for 7 to 90 days, we’ll make sure you are top of mind the moment they decide to make their purchase. It’s our job to find and deliver them. It’s your job to close the deal.

It’s easy, fast and takes no effort! The MORE-TO-WIN™ team does all the work for your dealership. We identify the buyers, do the marketing and drive them to your store. Your exclusive areaprovides you with the best chance of capturing sales for lower cost than your current digital or traditional marketing programs. You just close the sales.
